What is Emotional Intelligence?

Your ability to understand manage and use our emotions in positive way to communicate effectively through empathizing with others, overcoming challenges, defusing conflict and relieving stress.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is a measure of relative intelligence. Whilst EQ is your ability to manage my own emotions and the emotions of others.

Why is it important?

An example of why EQ is important for instance in a workplace would be for the optimal function of the work environment. Because your EQ is the management of yourself and others, striving to help people be their optimal self serves beneficially to the performance of a team.
